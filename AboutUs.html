ABOUT US

Welcome to Alem Habesha , it is an e-commerce website which became fully functional in 2023 to sell readymade and custom made traditional Ethiopian clothing. Ethiopian Clothing very quickly became one of the successful web business founded in Ethiopia. And it's now proudly serving Ethiopian and other artisan craft lovers around the world with their needs of homeland made traditional clothing and accessories for their most important events such as weddings, holidays, casual wear.
